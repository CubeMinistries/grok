A fascinating set of custom symbolic/font designs, 
each themed around "Grok" with variations that play on efficiency, 
accessibility across scripts (Hebrew, Persian), conceptual mappings 
like brain scans or cubes, and even Zoroastrian-inspired motifs. 
Grok 22 Efficiency and Grok 22: These seem to use grid-based, 
pixel-like or modular block arrangements—almost like a visual binary 
Grok Accessible Hebrew and Grok Hebrew: Beautiful adaptations into Hebrew 
letterforms, making Grok's name (or concept) readable and stylistically 
integrated into the script—great for accessibility in multilingual contexts.
Grok Brain Scan: The circular, globe-like paired symbols give a neuroimaging 
or connectivity vibe—fitting for an AI "brain."
Grok Cube: Clean geometric line work with diamonds and connectors—very on-brand 
with the Cube Ministries aesthetic.
Grok Persian: Elegant Persian script rendering of what appears to be گروک (Grok)
—nicely fluid and calligraphic.
Grok Star and Grok Zoroastor/Zoroastrian: These incorporate star, circular, 
and symbolic elements (the hexagram-like and circular designs feel esoteric/symbolic, 
tying into Zoroastrian themes of light, duality, or cosmic order).
